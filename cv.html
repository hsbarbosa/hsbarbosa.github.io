Profile

Research focus

My research focuses on how people move through cities and how mobility systems shape inequality, opportunity, and urban wellbeing. I develop computational methods using large-scale behavioural data to generate interpretable evidence for transport and planning policy.

I collaborate across computer science, urban studies, and applied network science, with work published in leading interdisciplinary journals.
