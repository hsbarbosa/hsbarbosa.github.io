Using computational and data-driven methods to understand human mobility and urban systems

My work focuses on the structure and dynamics of movement in cities, combining network science, large-scale data analysis, and complex systems modelling to support more equitable and resilient urban futures.

Google Scholar Exeter Experts ORCID GitHub

I am a Senior Lecturer at the University of Exeter. My research examines human mobility and urban systems using computational methods and large-scale behavioural data, with a focus on interpretable evidence for transport, inequality, and planning.
